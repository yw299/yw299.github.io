About me

Yuhang Wang was born in Jurong, China in 1998. She grew up in China and then went to the United States for college. She is currently a sophomore studying Information Science at Cornell University. She loves designing and is a huge fan of Fine Art Photography. Her favorite photographers are Richard Rinaldi, Leslie Zhang, and Solomon Mortimer. She loves old stuff, such as movies, photos, clothes, songs, because she feels a sense of connection to the past. Yuhang believes designing is a process of instilling empathy and expressing oneself. She hopes you enjoy her website.

Thank you for taking the time to look through my work! Please don't hesitate to reach out to me at yw299@cornell.edu for any questions or comments! See more about me on: LinkedIn and Medium.
